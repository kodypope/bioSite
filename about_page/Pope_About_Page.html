Ryan enjoys escape rooms, where you are locked in a room, although not actually locked in, and you solve a series of games, puzzles, or challenges to get out of the game. All must be done typically within one hour. You beat the game and escape if you leave the room within one hour. If the timer runs out before you can get out, you lose. However, part of the fun is that people watch you on a computer screen through cameras stationed throughout the room. They are there to help guide you on your quest to get out of the room. Part of the fun is never asking for help; they are there if needed. Also, depending on how challenging the room is, part of the fun is that you usually get a set number of questions to ask that do not count against your score. Typically, it is three questions. If you must ask more than three questions to get out within the hour, technically, you did not beat the room. Ryan also really enjoys Korean barbecue.
